What is Cookie?

Cookie is a small mulitipurpose software being developed by students. It has got many useful application built such as Cookie Learning (a programming learning application), Cookie VA (beta, a virtual assisstant app for improved and faster network surfing), Cookie Classroom Trial(a virtual classroom) and many many more.


We build this application for joy and fun which makes programming much more fun. It helps us to learn a lot and do much more stuff. Our major goal is to make many useful open source application which can help to make everyone's life easy. We would love to create awesome applications for you. If you would like to support us the easiest way is to write a review about our applications at Cookie Review or write to us at cookielearning@gmail.com.


We are open to all your ideas and will love to work on it. Please share your ideas and become a contributor of cookie. We would love to have you in our team.
